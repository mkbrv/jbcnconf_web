Talks Day (Monday-Tuesday):

Centre Comercial Gran Via 2 Avinguda de la Granvia, 75 08908 L'Hospitalet de Llobregat, Barcelona

Parking

If you are coming with your own car, you can use the Shopping Center Parking with a special deal for the conference : 10 EUR for all day. You only need to take a ticket as usual when you enter the parking and before leaving you have to go to the Central Office in the Parking and they will give you a new ticket after paying 10 € that you will use to leave.

>> How to get here? <<

Gran Via 2 is at the heart of a new zone under expansion. Access the centre from the south of Barcelona via the Av. de la Gran Via. This is the same area that is hosting the Mobile World Congress every February.

Access by metro: station Europa | Fira, Lines: 8, 9S: 35 minutes from the airport, Terminals 1 & 2.
